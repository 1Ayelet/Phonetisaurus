41  /*
42  Read in pairs of sequences of the form SEQ1 and SEQ2 and
43  transform them into an FST that encodes all possible
44  alignments between the symbols in the two sequences.
45  Note that this may include a combination of multi-symbol
46  subsequences depending on user specifications.
47 
48  This is achieved by simply generating the entire alignment
49  graph during a single nested loop through the two input
50  sequences that are to be aligned.
51 
52  The user may optionally specify whether to allow deletions
53  for SEQ1 or SEQ2, as well as a maximum subsequence length
54  for each sequence.
55 
56  This class does not implement any lattice pruning or printing
57  methods. A combination of the LatticePruner and FstPathFinder
58  classes may be used to achieve this a-la phonetisaurus-align.cpp.
59  */
